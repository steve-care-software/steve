Register an Identity

Registering an identity consists of reserving a username and generating a private key assigned to that username. The private key will be encrypted using your choosen passwword. The private key is generated using 24 words that will be shown to you after submitting the form.

You need to write down the words because if you lose your password, you'll need them to recover your account.

The username and password are mandatory in order to register an identity.

Please note that the seed word order below is important. When recovering your account you'll need to provide them in the right order to reset your identity's password.

The private key's seed words are:

  1. abandon
  2. about
  3. army
  4. basket
  5. blanket
  6. board
  7. budget
  8. chat
  9. consider
  10. crystal
  11. detect
  12. drum

  15. frame

  17. hub

  20. make
  21. myself
  22. oppose
  23. peace
  24. priority
