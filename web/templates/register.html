Register

Registering an identity consists of reserving a username and generating a private key assigned to that username. The private key will be encrypted using your choosen passwword. The private key is generated using 24 words that will be shown to you after submitting the form.

You need to write down the words because if you lose your password, you'll need them to recover your account.

Please note that the order is important, the words must be provided in the same order in order to Recover your account, in case your lose your password.

The private key's seed words are:

  1. abandon
  2. about
  3. army
  4. basket
  5. blanket
  6. board
  7. budget
  8. chat
  9. consider
  10. crystal
  11. detect
  12. drum
  13. energy
  14. february
  15. frame
  16. hair
  17. hub
  18. iron
  19. law
  20. make
  21. myself
  22. oppose
  23. peace
  24. priority
